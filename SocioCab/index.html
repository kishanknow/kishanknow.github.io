Services

Enjoy rideshare services spanning micro,mini and sedan cabs and autos.Also, we've got you the solo cab section!With ultra affordable ride insurance and a fleet of reliable vehicles, we're at your service!

0 cancellation fees

We ensure that you don't have to pay us in case the driver cancels your ride.Also,we assure you no cancellation from our side on a prior booking of 15 minutes.

Enviroment

We employ a strategic plantation policy that makes sure that we function as a net carbon zero company.Any rides you're making with us are never a burden on the Planet.

Match maker

Our rideshare match-finder is here to help you find amazing people to share your rides with.Team up with people who share your sport fandom, have the same foodie cravings and talk your way through the hustle and bustle of the city.

Safety first

We have a separate network of cabs and autos that are 'Only For Women'. Women can avail our regular services or can opt for such 'Only For Women' cabs.

Special service

We offer our regular users the facility of using solo cabs at just the same price as a share-cab if they're ill and produce a medical certificate. We make sure we're there for you and also make sure there's no risk of diseases in our rides.
